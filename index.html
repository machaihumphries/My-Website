What I want to acheive and help people with is becoming more creative and diverse with creating entertainment like stories, movies and more! Most media these days to me are really similer to eachother with only a mostly a few things having creative techniques and things added in to be brand new and fresh. I want to create a website where people can express all sorts of creative things, from art, videos, stories, comics, poems, ideas, games, to even fan art and supporting franchises. Now with all these subjects it would be hard keeping track of ALL of them, so I thought of spliting all these different subjects into sections, so they can be easy to navigate and travel threw.
